Popular Destinations

NALANDA UNIVERSITY

Incredible Beacon of Ancient Knowledge. Founded in the 5th century, it was one of the first residential universities in the world.

BODH GAYA

Trace the Footsteps of the Buddha. Home to the sacred Bodhi Tree where Buddha attained enlightenment.

RAJGIR

Ancient city with rich history. Known for hot springs, hills, and Buddhist & Jain heritage.

PATNA

Modern city with spiritual magnet. Home to historical sites like Golghar, Mahavir Mandir, and museums.

MADHUBANI

Pride of Mithilanchal. One can also see Mithila painting as a movement that broke the caste barrier.

PAWAPURI JAL MANDIR

The Jalmandir. The main deity of the temple is the very old “Charan Paduka” of Lord Mahavira.
